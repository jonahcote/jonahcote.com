The hackathon was a wild place--I'd never been in the same building with so many other people passionate about tech before. After a bit of brainstorming, I settled on a project to leverage competitive teenagers to help improve the environment. The program, EcoBot, could be added to channels of the text client Discord and display a leaderboard of the members of that friend group. Different environmentally-friendly actions earned points on the leaderboard.

The program itself was written primarily in Python, with JavaScript connecting the logic to Discord's API. The leaderboard itself was stored in an SQL database on Azure. After lots of StackOverflow and a power nap, the bot worked in time for judging.
